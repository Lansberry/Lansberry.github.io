# Family Fury

*A family-themed fighting game. Mortal Kombat energy, Pittsburgh soul.*

*Last updated: June 19, 2026*

---

## Concept

Four-player family fighter. Each character is a member of the Lansberry household — with moves, energy, and finishers that are unmistakably them. Think Mortal Kombat character select screen but the roster is your family. The tone is warm chaos, not mean. Every character wins in their own way.

The name has iterated: Family Kombat → Family Fury. **Family Fury** is the working title.

---

## The Roster

---

### Finnble
*"Pathfinder" | Age 3 | The Curious Destroyer*

**Color:** Orange
**Energy:** Boundless, erratic, deeply earnest

**Lore:** Born in the quiet hours of a Tuesday night under a returning crescent moon. He disappeared briefly after birth and came back unharmed — the hospital had no explanation. That's just Finn. He finds his way back. A Water Tiger and a Taurus: equally capable of charging through a wall or sitting very still and watching you from across the room with a look that means he already knows.

**Fighting Style:** Questions-as-weapons. Affection as a stun move. Absolutely will not stop.

**Signature Moves:**
- *Bumblenums Barrage* — flings stuffed animals in a rapid combo
- *Why? Why? Why?* — a three-hit stun combo of unanswerable questions
- *Magic Wand Druid Shift* — turns opponent's weapon into a plant or small dinosaur
- *Superhero Landing* — leaps from offscreen and lands in a full hero pose (brief invincibility)

**Finisher:** *The Forky Protocol* — opens chest cavity to reveal a glowing book-and-magnet core. Launches letter magnets and picture books at opponent while asking a rapid-fire stream of questions. Opponent is knocked out mid-answer.

### Mom
*"Holly" | The Grounded Force | Working name TBD*

**Color:** Yellow
**Energy:** Rooted, capable, quietly the most dangerous person in the room

**Lore:** Rosie the Riveter if Rosie also kept a garden, knew how to fix the fence herself, and had a baby on her hip the whole time. Pioneer Woman energy. The kind of person who solves the actual problem while everyone else is still identifying it.

**Fighting Style:** Garden tools. Practical. Efficient. No wasted movement.

**Signature Moves:**
- *Trowel Strike* — fast low-sweep with a garden trowel
- *Bug Drop* — deploys Bug (companion) as a distraction/damage move
- *Rivet Cannon* — Rosie mode activated: shoulder charge with rivet-gun ranged attack
- *The Look* — a single sustained stare that slowly drains opponent's will to fight

**Finisher:** *Pioneer Wrath* — summons full garden equipment storm. Rakes, shovels, watering cans, seed packets. Ends with a single raised eyebrow and a hand on hip while opponent is buried.

> **Note:** Holly's fighter name ("Mom" is placeholder) is still open — something better than Mom but that captures the same energy.

---

### Stormborn
*"Ben" | Benjamin Bodhi | The Weather Event*

**Color:** Stormy blue-grey / lightning yellow
**Energy:** Unpredictable. Operatic. Overwhelming.

**Lore:** Born during a tornado warning. Sirens. 70mph winds. 400,000 homes without power. Lightning over the city. He arrived anyway, first cry cutting right through the storm. He is Mini Thor. He is colic as a superpower. Wood Snake by Chinese zodiac: graceful and strategic underneath the chaos, always shedding to grow. A Taurus like his brother — stubborn, earth-anchored, but the storm got there first.

**Fighting Style:** Elemental weather attacks. Tears as projectiles. Pure chaos energy.

**Signature Moves:**
- *Colicky Tempest* — sustained wail that generates a damaging sound wave
- *Typhoon Tears* — projectile attack: concentrated tears fired in a spread pattern
- *Tornado Tantrum* — spinning attack that pulls opponent in then releases them
- *The Squall* — passive aura that slightly reduces all nearby opponent speed

**Finisher:** *Storm of the Three Rivers* — lightning cracks the screen, three river tributaries flood the arena, Ben stands in the center completely dry and unbothered while opponent is swept away. Final beat: a single very loud cry. Screen shakes.

### Dad
*"Nick" | The Architect / Dad.exe | TBD — see options below*

**Color:** Green
**Energy:** Goofy. Competent. Suspiciously prepared for this situation.

**Lore:** Builds systems. Makes movies. Has extremely strong opinions about Salesforce field naming conventions. Cried at the end of Toy Story 3. Would absolutely stop a street fight to ask if anyone needs a spreadsheet about it. Pittsburgh born, Three Rivers in the blood — the point where things converge. His CliftonStrengths include Futuristic and Ideation, which means he's always three steps ahead and slightly distracted by his own ideas.

**Fighting Style:** Improvised. Architectural. Occasionally Dad-joke-based.

**Signature Moves:**
- *Deploy to Production* — fires a rapid burst of code commits at opponent (each one does a little damage, there are a lot of them)
- *The Dad Joke* — opponent is briefly stunned by a truly terrible pun
- *Scope Creep* — attack that starts small and grows dramatically mid-animation
- *Star Wars Reference* — lightsaber appears for exactly one move before he puts it away ("I shouldn't have done that")

**Finisher — Option A: "The Architect"**
Blueprint drops from above, outlines the entire arena. Builds a structure around the opponent in real-time. Final line: *"The system is live."* Opponent is trapped inside the diagram.

**Finisher — Option B: "Dad.exe Has Crashed"**
Dad.exe runs fine until one too many simultaneous requests (kids asking questions, Slack notification, someone needs a snack). Blue screen appears. Massive system crash animation. Opponent is caught in the crash. Dad reboots in 3 seconds, totally fine.

> **Your call on the name and finisher.** Both options lean into different true things about you. The Architect is the professional identity. Dad.exe is the LANSBERRY.EXE vibe. Either works — or suggest something else entirely.

## Arena

**Three Rivers Arena** — Pittsburgh at dusk. The Confluence in the background where the rivers meet. Lighting shifts from golden hour to lightning-storm depending on which characters are fighting. A K'Nex sculpture in the corner that you can knock opponents into.

---

## Design Notes

- **Tone:** Warm chaos. Nobody is the villain. The kids fight like kids, Mom fights like someone who has handled worse, Dad fights like someone who is doing his best.
- **Visual style:** Mortal Kombat character select screen energy, but the color palette is warm — oranges, yellows, greens, Pittsburgh steel-and-river tones.
- **Music:** Pittsburgh-themed. Something with a Three Rivers groove. Open question.
- **Future characters:** Cousins? Grandparents? The dog?
