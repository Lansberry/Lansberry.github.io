### Questions to Ask Every Roofer
- Will you do a repair assessment first, or quote both repair AND full replacement?
- What's the shingle brand, grade, and warranty (labor + material separately)?
- Are you pulling a permit?
- What's the workmanship warranty?
- Do you handle insurance claims if storm damage is involved?
- How do you handle decking replacement if there's rot underneath?

> Pro tip: Ask every contractor to assess repair vs. replace — a good roofer will tell you honestly which one makes financial sense rather than defaulting to the bigger job.
